01
Official Communication Channel

All purchases, enquiries, and order communications take place exclusively via Instagram Direct Message at @ossuary.atelier. This is the only official channel. No transaction is confirmed until you have received a written confirmation from us over DM.

If you have a question about an item, a complaint, or a concern after purchase — DM us. We respond within 24 hours. Do not attempt to resolve issues through Instagram comments or third-party platforms.
02
Payment

Payment is required in full before the item is packaged or dispatched. Accepted payment methods will be confirmed over DM at the time of purchase — currently we accept bank transfer (Thailand) and PromptPay. Payment details are shared privately via DM only.

An item is not reserved until payment is received and confirmed. If payment is not received within 48 hours of a purchase being agreed over DM, the item returns to available stock without notice.

All prices are listed in Thai Baht (฿). Shipping cost, if applicable, is confirmed before payment is requested.

03
Shipping & Delivery

Orders are packaged and dispatched within 3–5 business days of confirmed payment. You will receive a tracking number via DM once your order has been handed to the carrier.

We currently ship within Thailand. International shipping may be available on request — ask over DM before purchasing. Any additional costs for international delivery will be disclosed and agreed before payment.

Delivery timelines after dispatch depend on the carrier and destination. Ossuary Atelier is not responsible for delays caused by the postal service, customs processing, or incorrect addresses provided by the buyer. Please confirm your shipping address carefully before purchase is finalised.

04
Item Condition & Disclosure

Every item sold by Ossuary Atelier is pre-owned vintage or secondhand clothing. All items are inspected, cleaned, and photographed before listing. Condition is disclosed honestly on each item's listing and story page — including any flaws, repairs, or signs of age.

By purchasing, you acknowledge that you have read the item description and accept that vintage and secondhand garments carry the natural characteristics of age and prior use. Minor inconsistencies in colour, texture, and construction are inherent to the nature of the pieces we sell — they are not defects.

Measurements are provided for every item. It is the buyer's responsibility to check measurements against their own body before purchasing. We are happy to answer any questions about fit, fabric, or condition over DM before you commit.
05
Returns & Exchanges
All sales are final. We do not accept returns or offer exchanges on vintage or secondhand items. Once payment is confirmed and the item is dispatched, the sale is complete.

We take condition disclosure seriously and describe every item accurately and in detail. If you have any doubts about a piece — its condition, measurements, or suitability — please ask us over DM before purchasing. We would rather answer twenty questions than have a buyer receive something that doesn't work for them.

The only exception to the no-returns policy is if an item arrives in a condition that is materially and significantly different from how it was described in the listing — for example, undisclosed damage that was present before dispatch. In that case, contact us via DM within 48 hours of delivery with photographs, and we will assess the situation and respond within 24 hours. Resolution may include a partial refund or store credit at our discretion.

06
Archive Items & QR Story Tags

Certain items are designated as Archive pieces — items with verified provenance, unusual labels, or significant story value. These items include a QR hangtag linking to a dedicated story page documenting the item's origin, era, and history.

The story page is a permanent record of what we know about the piece up to the point of sale. It will remain live after purchase. Buyers are welcome to share their own chapter of the item's life with us — we may feature it on the story page or our blog with permission — but there is no obligation to do so.

Please preserve the QR hangtag when possible. It is part of what makes the piece an Archive item.

Questions & Contact

DM us on Instagram at @ossuary.atelier. We respond within 24 hours. For order-specific issues, please have your item ID and order date ready when you message.
