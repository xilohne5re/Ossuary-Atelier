We research the label, date the construction, note the fabric, and write the origin story — in first person, past tense, specific. Then we publish it to a dedicated page and encode the URL into a QR tag attached to the garment.

Every piece leaves us with its QR tag attached. Scan it any time — on the day it arrives, or five years later. The page stays live. The story doesn't disappear when the item changes hands. You become the next chapter.
